FAQ
Frequently Asked Questions
What is ABHA?▼
ABHA (Ayushman Bharat Health Account) is a 14-digit unique health ID issued by the National Health Authority of India. It links all your health records in one place.
Is my data safe?▼
Yes. All your data is encrypted using AES-256. Only you can grant or revoke access to your health records. CureNet complies with DPDP Act 2023.
How do I share records with a doctor?▼
Go to "Share with Doctor" on the home screen, show the QR code to your doctor, and approve their request. Access expires in 30 minutes.
What is ABHAy AI?▼
ABHAy is your personal health assistant. Ask about your medications, past records, or general health questions in Hindi or English. Available 24/7.
How to create ABHA?▼
Tap "Register" on the login screen and choose Aadhaar Number or Mobile Number. Verify with OTP and your ABHA will be created for free.
Can I use CureNet offline?▼
Yes. Your last synced health records are available offline. You will need internet to fetch new records or grant doctor access.
